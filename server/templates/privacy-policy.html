Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

ChefSpAIce ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your username and password (stored securely using encryption). If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive basic profile information from those services.

Food Inventory Data: We store information about food items you add to your inventory, including names, quantities, storage locations, and expiration dates. This data is essential for the app's core functionality.

Kitchen Equipment: We store your selected kitchen appliances and equipment to provide relevant recipe suggestions.

Recipe & Meal Plan Data: We store recipes you save and meal plans you create.

Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the ChefSpAIce service
  • To generate personalized recipe recommendations based on your inventory
  • To send expiration reminders and notifications (with your permission)
  • To process your subscription and payments through Stripe
  • To improve our app and develop new features

AI-Powered Features

ChefSpAIce uses OpenAI's technology to generate recipes and provide kitchen assistance. When you use AI features, relevant data (such as your inventory items or questions) is sent to OpenAI to generate responses. OpenAI processes this data according to their privacy policy and does not use it to train their models.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers using industry-standard encryption. We use PostgreSQL databases hosted on secure cloud infrastructure. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and are never stored in plain text.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Stripe: For secure payment processing. Stripe handles all payment information according to PCI compliance standards.
  • OpenAI: For AI-powered recipe generation and kitchen assistance.
  • Apple/Google Sign-In: For optional social authentication.
  • USDA FoodData Central: For nutrition information lookup.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Export your data (inventory, recipes) in common formats
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of non-essential notifications

Children's Privacy

ChefSpAIce is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Push Notifications

With your permission, we send push notifications for food expiration reminders. You can disable these at any time in your device settings or within the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
